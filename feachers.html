We believe in simple pricing. Our monthly fees are based on the number of rooms you have registered and the version you choose (Starter or Pro). A fee is also charged per text/SMS you send through Sirvoy (optional service). We charge no start-up fee and no commission on your bookings. You top up your account at any time and with the amount of your choice. If no payment is made - the account will simply be closed. If your business is based in Ireland, our prices are subject to 23% VAT.


If you connect to booking channels (Booking.com, TripAdvisor, etc.), they will likely charge their commission. Enabling online payments might likewise incur fees from such third parties, please check with the payment provider.


This feature may not be compatible with tax legislation in your area.
