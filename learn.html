Hotel Channel Manager

Connect Easily with Hundreds of OTAs and Booking Websites.

Sirvoy a powerful hotel channel manager that has direct connections with all major booking channels, such as Booking.com, Expedia.com, Hotels.com, Agoda.com, Hostelworld, HotelSpecials, TripAdvisor Instant Booking and Google Hotel Ads.

Using our iCal import/export feature, you can automatically keep your availability up-to-date on Airbnb, Homeaway, VRBO, 9flats, and other vacation rental sites that support the iCal format. If you want to connect with even more channels, you can also connect your Sirvoy account to third-party channel managers such as Myallocator.com and SiteMinder.

Work with Multiple Channels – without the Hassle


To collect and organize hotel reservations received from several different channels can be very time consuming, so it can really be a life saver to use a hotel channel manager. With a hotel channel manager you get increased revenue, but without the constant headache of keeping availability up-to-date and worrying about overbookings.


Hotel software with a Built-In Channel Manager


When using our hotel software all your reservations will automatically be collected, and your availability kept up-to-date, while you can give your full attention to other things. If you’re after a well designed, user-friendly property management system, with a seamlessly integrated channel manager, then you will definitely be pleased with Sirvoy hotel booking system.
